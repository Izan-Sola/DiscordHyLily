# DiscordHyLily
Discord bot to talk with Lily!

## Features:

- Ping her or reply to her with a text message and she will reply with a text message.
- She can send audio messages if you either reply to her with an voice messsage or use the command /audiolily [message]
- She can join and chat in calls (it doesn't work too well) by using the command /voice join, and leave by using /voice leave. 
  You can speak to her in call by either starting or ending your sentence with her name, i.e "Lily, how are you" or "How are you Lily?".
  Or you can just ping her or reply to her with a text message and she will respond in the call too.
- She as access to a database with information about the Hytale Wiki, so she can answer questions about knowledge that can be found in it.
- She as a memory database. She can remember facts about users or the server when mentioned. You can also force her to add/update/remove something from memory by just explicity telling her.

Please note that she is just a lil cutie bot, she ain't too smart, so be patient with her if she struggles with something.

## Commands:

- /voice join: Use while in an audio channel to make her join.
- /voice leave: Use while in an audio channel to make her leave.
- /aboutlily: Display information about Lily.
- /audiolily [message]: To make her respond with a voice message.
